Everyday Life

Polite, practical language for the small exchanges that make a day work.

Cultural Note

Politeness often lives in the verb. A direct translation of “I want” may sound abrupt in Portuguese service encounters. Queria… — literally “I would like…” — makes a request softer. A greeting before the request also matters: Bom dia. Queria uma água, por favor.

Listen & use

  • Queria uma água, por favor.I would like a water, please.
  • Quanto custa?How much is it?
  • Onde fica a estação?Where is the station?
  • Pode repetir?Can you repeat?
