Session 2: Everyday Life

Learning goals

  • Order something in a café
  • Ask for prices and directions
  • Understand basic numbers and times
  • Use polite requests

Essential phrases

PortugueseEnglishUse
Queria um café, por favor.I would like a coffee, please.Polite and natural
Quanto custa?How much does it cost?Shops and services
Onde fica…?Where is…?Asking for directions
Pode ajudar-me?Can you help me?Formal/polite
A que horas?At what time?Appointments and transport
Não percebo.I don’t understand.Very useful for beginners
Pode repetir?Can you repeat?Polite request

Activity 1: Café role-play

A: Bom dia. Queria um café e uma água, por favor.
B: Mais alguma coisa?
A: Não, obrigado/a. Quanto é?

Activity 2: Everyday survival

Choose one situation and prepare two sentences:

  • asking where the train station is
  • asking the price of an item
  • asking someone to repeat
  • asking what time a service opens

Teacher note

Keep the session highly practical. Use real menus, transport images or screenshots supplied during the live class.
