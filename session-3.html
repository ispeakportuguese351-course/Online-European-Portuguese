Living & Working

Professional language, social conventions and the spaces between formality and familiarity.

Cultural Note

Relationships often precede business. Forms of address can be more formal than in Finland, and brief small talk may come before the practical purpose of a meeting. Coffee and meals can be important social spaces. Politeness is often conveyed indirectly through tone, verb choice and phrasing.

None of these conventions is absolute. Use them as observation prompts, not fixed rules: age, sector, region and the relationship between speakers all matter.

Listen & respond

  • Trabalho em…I work in…
  • Podemos marcar uma reunião?Can we arrange a meeting?
  • Qual é a sua disponibilidade?What is your availability?
  • Envio-lhe um e-mail.I will send you an email.

Object & language

Describe one visible detail before interpreting the object. This slow-looking prompt introduces vocabulary for shape, material and professional museum conversations.
