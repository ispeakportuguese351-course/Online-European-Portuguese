Session 3: Living and Working

Learning goals

  • Handle a basic professional introduction
  • Arrange a meeting or appointment
  • Use polite workplace expressions
  • Recognise key Portuguese cultural conventions

Essential phrases

PortugueseEnglishUse
Trabalho em…I work in…Professional introduction
Podemos marcar uma reunião?Can we arrange a meeting?Workplace
Qual é a sua disponibilidade?What is your availability?Formal
Envio-lhe um e-mail.I’ll send you an email.Formal
Até amanhã.See you tomorrow.Common closing
Com licença.Excuse me.To enter, interrupt or pass
Desculpe o atraso.Sorry I’m late.Useful social phrase

Activity 1: Professional introduction

Bom dia. Chamo-me ________. Trabalho em ________. Estou em Portugal há ________.

Activity 2: Arrange a meeting

Create a short dialogue using:

Podemos marcar uma reunião? · Na terça-feira? · Às dez horas? · Perfeito. Até terça-feira.

Cultural reflection

  • Forms of address may be more formal than in Finland.
  • Small talk often precedes business.
  • Meals and coffee can be important social spaces.
  • Politeness is often expressed indirectly through tone and phrasing.

Teacher note

End with a five-minute recap and a short feedback prompt: What can you now say in Portuguese that you could not say before?
