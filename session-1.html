Greetings · Introductions · Pronunciation

Session 1: First Encounters

Learning goals

  • Greet people appropriately
  • Introduce yourself
  • Recognise key sounds of European Portuguese
  • Use basic polite expressions

Essential phrases

PortugueseEnglishNote
OláHelloNeutral and widely used
Bom diaGood morningUsually until lunchtime
Boa tardeGood afternoonFrom lunchtime onwards
Boa noiteGood evening / good nightUsed in the evening
Chamo-me…My name is…Literally “I call myself”
Sou finlandês / finlandesaI am FinnishMasculine / feminine form
Muito prazerNice to meet youUseful in formal and informal contexts
Obrigado / ObrigadaThank youSpeaker identifies as masculine / feminine

Activity 1: Introduce yourself

Complete and say aloud:

Olá. Chamo-me ________. Sou ________. Vivo em ________. Muito prazer.

Activity 2: Pronunciation focus

Notice the reduced vowels in European Portuguese. Practise slowly:

Portugal · Lisboa · obrigado · boa tarde · português

Teacher note

Use 10 minutes for greetings, 15 minutes for pronunciation, 20 minutes for paired introductions, and 15 minutes for cultural discussion.
