First Encounters

How language, tone and attention shape a first meeting in Portugal.

Cultural Note

A greeting is more than a word. In Portugal, the choice between olá, bom dia and a more formal form of address helps establish the relationship. First encounters may feel slightly more formal than in Finland. Observe the other person, mirror their tone and let familiarity develop naturally.
